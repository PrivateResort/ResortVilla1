About Us

Come and have fun while celebrating at Villa Aurora Private Resort! We offer a stunning venue where you can host parties for special occasions such as anniversaries, weddings, birthdays, and reunions. Additionally, we provide amenities such as a swimming pool, karaoke, and a billiards area, ensuring that you and your guests will have an enjoyable and entertaining experience! Our resort is conveniently located in Kingfisher, Angono, Rizal.
